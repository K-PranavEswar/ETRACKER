About SmartExpense AI

SmartExpense AI is an intelligent expense tracking system designed to help users manage and understand their finances more effectively.

💡 What is this App?

SmartExpense AI is a web-based financial management system built using Flask. It allows users to track expenses, monitor budgets, and gain insights into their spending behavior.

🎯 Purpose

  • Track daily and monthly expenses
  • Monitor and control spending
  • Improve financial awareness
  • Provide smart financial insights

⚙️ Features

  • Expense tracking system
  • Budget management
  • Analytics dashboard
  • Secure login system
  • CSV export reports

🚀 Future Scope

  • AI-based spending suggestions
  • Mobile app integration
  • Smart notifications
  • Advanced analytics & predictions
  • Cloud sync across devices

🌍 Why this App?

Managing money is essential in modern life. This app helps users gain control over their finances, reduce unnecessary spending, and build better financial habits.
